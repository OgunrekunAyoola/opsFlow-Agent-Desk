# Integrations

OpsFlowAI integrates with your existing support channels and business tools.

## Email Setup

Most teams start by connecting their support email.

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Configure Forwarding">
    Set up email forwarding from your support address to your unique OpsFlow address.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2} title="Verify DNS">
    Add SPF/DKIM records to your DNS provider to allow OpsFlow to send replies on your behalf.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## CRM Integrations

Sync customer data from Shopify, Stripe, and Salesforce.

<Callout type="info">
  CRM data allows the EnrichmentAgent to provide critical context like lifetime value and recent orders.
</Callout>

## Billing

Connect Stripe to enable ActionAgent to perform autonomous refunds and subscription cancellations.
