# Getting Started

Welcome to OpsFlowAI. This guide will help you get up and running with your autonomous support agent in minutes.

## Overview

OpsFlowAI is an agent-oriented support platform. Unlike traditional helpdesks, OpsFlow puts AI agents at the center of the workflow, allowing you to resolve tickets with 90% less human intervention.

<Callout type="info">
  OpsFlowAI is currently in public beta. All features are available for testing.
</Callout>

## Installation

To start using OpsFlowAI, you need to install the agent desk and connect your support channels.

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Create an account">
    Sign up for a free account at [opsflow.ai/signup](https://opsflow.ai/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step number={2} title="Connect your email">
    Go to Settings > Integrations and connect your support email (e.g., support@yourcompany.com).
  </Step>
  <Step number={3} title="Import your knowledge base">
    Upload your documentation or sync with your Notion workspace to train your agents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Agent Pipeline

Every ticket flows through our multi-agent pipeline:

1. **TriageAgent**: Classifies and prioritizes.
2. **EnrichmentAgent**: Gathers context from CRM and previous tickets.
3. **RAGAgent**: Fetches relevant knowledge base snippets.
4. **ResponseAgent**: Drafts the final reply.
